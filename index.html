TemplateOps Draft Manager

TemplateOps Draft Manager helps prepare Etsy draft listings for digital products such as Notion templates, spreadsheet trackers and printable PDF kits.

Purpose

This app is used by the shop owner to create and review draft listings before they are manually checked in Etsy Seller. It is not a public marketplace tool, SaaS product or buyer-facing application.

What It Does

  • Prepares draft listings for digital downloads.
  • Uploads listing images and product files for review.
  • Reads shop and listing metadata needed for verification.
  • Keeps publishing decisions manual inside Etsy Seller.

Requested Access

  • listings_r to verify listing metadata.
  • listings_w to create and update draft listings.
  • shops_r to identify the connected shop.

Publishing Policy

The app does not automatically publish listings. Drafts are reviewed manually by the seller before anything goes live on Etsy.
